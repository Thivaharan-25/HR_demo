==============================================================================
  PEOPLECORE — FULL DEMO PRESENTATION GUIDE
  Step-by-Step Walkthrough for Presenters
==============================================================================

TOTAL ESTIMATED TIME: 20-30 minutes (adjustable per audience)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — OPENING HOOK  (2 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "How much time does your HR team lose every week chasing attendance sheets,
    manually running payroll, and figuring out who is actually ready for a
    promotion? Most companies still treat HR as a back-office function.
    PeopleCore changes that — it turns your people data into a strategic asset."

  POINT OUT (on landing page):
    -> The animated parallax hero showing real screenshots of every module
    -> Headline: "The Intelligent People Infrastructure"
    -> Sub-copy: biometric attendance, AI-driven talent DNA, payroll compliance

  KEY MESSAGE: PeopleCore is NOT just an HRMS. It is an intelligence layer
  for your entire workforce.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — PRICING AND VALUE PROPOSITION  (2 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  SCROLL DOWN to the Pricing section on the landing page.

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "We have three tiers. Starter at $5/user/month gives small teams
    everything they need: attendance, leave, directory, and a self-service
    portal. Professional at $11 unlocks the full platform — Nexis AI,
    payroll integrations, and advanced analytics. Enterprise is custom for
    organisations that need white-labeling, SSO, and dedicated compliance."

  HIGHLIGHT:
    -> Starter  = affordable entry, no credit card risk
    -> Professional = the 'wow' tier — this is what you demo live
    -> 'Most Popular' badge on Professional
    -> $11 vs competitors: Workday $20+, BambooHR $8-$15, Rippling $10+

  KEY MESSAGE: Full AI-powered HR for less than a Netflix subscription
  per employee.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3 — ONBOARDING: SETUP WIZARD  (3 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CLICK "Start Free Trial" (hero button or pricing button — both work).

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "New customers land on a guided 9-step setup wizard — zero onboarding
    calls needed. Configure your company profile, upload your org chart,
    set up your first department, define leave policies. All of it in one
    sitting."

  POINT OUT:
    -> Left form: clear step-by-step inputs
    -> Right info panel: context-sensitive tips that change per step
    -> "How It All Connects" — visual map of all 8 modules showing they
       are one connected platform, not nine disconnected tools
    -> "PeopleCore at a Glance" stats: 500+ companies, 98% satisfaction
    -> Progress bar at the top tracking completion

  KEY MESSAGE: Any HR manager can be up and running in under an hour.
  No consultants. No migration nightmare.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 4 — ADMIN DASHBOARD OVERVIEW  (2 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CLICK "Enter Platform" and log in as Admin.

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "This is the admin command center. At a glance: total headcount,
    today's attendance rate, pending leave requests, and payroll status.
    Everything an HR director needs for their morning check-in — one screen."

  POINT OUT:
    -> Top KPI cards: Headcount, Present Today, Leave Pending, Payroll Status
    -> Sidebar navigation: 8 modules accessible instantly
    -> Dark/light mode toggle via the avatar in the bottom-left corner
    -> Real-time feel — everything updates live as you interact

  KEY MESSAGE: The daily driver for HR. Information density without
  information overload.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 5 — PEOPLE AND TALENT MODULE  (3 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  NAVIGATE to People, then click on an employee profile.

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "Every employee has a Talent DNA profile. Not just a name and job title —
    a living record of their skills, proficiency levels, certifications, and
    career history. The system knows what they can do today and what they
    need to reach the next level."

  POINT OUT:
    -> Skills section with proficiency badges: Beginner through Expert
    -> Department, manager chain, team membership visible at a glance
    -> Tenure and hire date on the profile

  THEN navigate to the Skills module:
    -> Show the Skills Requests queue: pending proficiency upgrades
    -> Click into a request: show From Level, To Level, evidence attached,
       and validator details
    -> Approve a request — watch it instantly update the employee profile

  KEY MESSAGE: Skills data is structured, validated, and always current.
  No more spreadsheet inventories that are 18 months out of date.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 6 — ATTENDANCE AND LEAVE  (2 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  NAVIGATE to Attendance, then to Leave.

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "Attendance is tracked with clock-in/clock-out — supports biometric
    hardware via API or the employee's own device. The leave module has full
    policy configuration: custom leave types, approval workflows, balance
    tracking, and a calendar view of who is out when."

  POINT OUT:
    -> Attendance log: timestamps, duration, status (Present/Late/Absent)
    -> Leave calendar: team visibility prevents scheduling conflicts
    -> Leave balance cards per employee
    -> Approve/Reject buttons for pending requests

  KEY MESSAGE: No more emailing managers to find out who is on leave.
  Everything is transparent and fully auditable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 7 — PAYROLL MODULE  (2 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  NAVIGATE to Payroll.

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "Payroll is directly connected to attendance data. Overtime is calculated
    automatically. Deductions for unexcused absences are applied at the
    source. One-click export to ADP, Xero, or QuickBooks. Compliance reports
    are generated automatically — no manual cross-referencing required."

  POINT OUT:
    -> Payroll summary table: gross/net breakdown per employee
    -> Integration badges: ADP, Xero
    -> Run Payroll button and the full audit trail
    -> Tax and statutory deduction columns

  KEY MESSAGE: Payroll that cannot make human errors because humans are
  not doing the calculations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 8 — NEXIS AI AGENT — THE INTELLIGENCE LAYER  (4 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CLICK the Nexis AI floating button in the bottom-right corner.

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "This is Nexis — our embedded AI co-pilot for HR. Not a generic chatbot.
    It has full read access to your org data and answers questions that would
    take an HR analyst hours to pull together."

  DEMO PROMPTS — TYPE THESE LIVE:

    1. "Who is at risk of leaving?"
       -> Attrition risk scores with reasoning: tenure, engagement signals

    2. "Who is ready for promotion in the engineering team?"
       -> Performance signals, skill levels, tenure — ranked candidates

    3. "What are the top skill gaps in the product team?"
       -> Current skills mapped against role requirements, gaps highlighted

    4. "Create a development plan for Sarah Chen"
       -> Structured 90-day growth plan built from her live profile

  WHAT TO SAY AFTER:
    "Every one of those answers used to require an HR analyst to pull data
    from four different systems, build a spreadsheet, and present it at a
    Monday meeting. Nexis does it in seconds — and it is always working
    with live data."

  KEY MESSAGE: Nexis turns your HR system from a record-keeper into a
  strategic advisor.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 9 — EMPLOYEE SELF-SERVICE  (3 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  SWITCH to the Employee App view.

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "Let us flip to the employee side. This is what your team members see —
    the same intelligence, but scoped to their own career and work day."

  POINT OUT:
    -> My Skills section: employee sees their own full skill profile
    -> Add Skill: type the skill name, set initial proficiency level —
       instant, no approval needed to add a skill to your own profile
    -> Upgrade Proficiency: click Upgrade on any skill — the modal shows
       the full Proficiency Progression Guide: what Beginner vs Intermediate
       vs Expert actually means, what you need to demonstrate to reach each
       level, and downloadable resources if they are attached
    -> Evidence upload (if required by that skill): text description + file
    -> Validator selection: if the skill requires manager validation, the
       employee picks from available managers. If 'direct manager' is set,
       it auto-routes to their assigned manager.
    -> After submitting, the request appears in the admin Skills queue.

  THEN show Nexis AI in the employee view:
    -> "What skills should I develop to get promoted?"
    -> "How does my attendance compare to my team this month?"

  KEY MESSAGE: Employees are active participants in their own development —
  not passive subjects in an HR database.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 10 — INTEGRATIONS  (1 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  NAVIGATE to Integrations (admin sidebar).

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "PeopleCore does not live in isolation. It plugs into tools you already
    use — Slack for notifications, Google Workspace for SSO, ADP and Xero
    for payroll, and more. Connected in one click. Disconnected just as
    easily."

  POINT OUT:
    -> Connected vs not-connected cards — cleanly aligned in the grid
    -> Status badges: Connected / Available
    -> Configure button for active integrations

  KEY MESSAGE: PeopleCore fits your existing tech stack.
  No rip-and-replace required.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 11 — CLOSING: THE FULL CIRCLE  (2 min)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  WHAT TO SAY:
    "Let me close the loop. A new hire joins your company. They are added
    in PeopleCore that same day. Their attendance starts tracking immediately.
    Their leave balance is calculated from day one. Their manager sets their
    skill baseline. Payroll includes them in the next run automatically.

    As they grow, they submit skill upgrade requests with evidence. Their
    manager validates it. Nexis AI tracks their readiness for the next level
    and flags them to the HR director before they even start looking elsewhere.

    When it is time for their annual review, everything is already documented.
    No chasing emails. No spreadsheet gymnastics. Just data-backed decisions,
    made fast.

    That is PeopleCore. One platform. From hire to retire."

  FINAL CALL TO ACTION:
    -> "Start your free trial today — Starter at $5/user/month."
    -> "Talk to us about Professional if you want the full AI suite."

  KEY OBJECTIONS AND RESPONSES
==============================================================================

  Q: "We already use BambooHR / Workday / SAP"
  A: PeopleCore can co-exist or replace. The AI layer alone is worth the
     switch — no legacy HRMS has Nexis-level intelligence built in.

  Q: "Is the data secure?"
  A: Role-based access, encrypted at rest and in transit, full audit trails
     on every sensitive action. Enterprise tier adds SSO and dedicated infra.

  Q: "How long does migration take?"
  A: The setup wizard has you live in under an hour. Employee data imports
     via CSV — no consultant required.

  Q: "Can employees see each other's salary?"
  A: No. The employee view is scoped strictly to their own data. Only admins
     see org-wide compensation.

  Q: "$11/month seems cheap — where is the catch?"
  A: There is no catch. Our infrastructure costs are under $0.50/user/month.
     We pass the efficiency to you and grow through volume.
